# Partner Readiness Checklist

Use this checklist for corridor partners, community partners, or launch affiliates.

## Operational readiness

- [ ] Partner point-of-contact confirmed.
- [ ] Escalation route tested.
- [ ] Service expectations documented.

## Compliance and trust

- [ ] Partner messaging aligns with platform scope and limits.
- [ ] Safety/escalation pathways are understood.
- [ ] Data/privacy expectations communicated.

## Launch coordination

- [ ] Launch date and support windows aligned.
- [ ] Issue reporting channel confirmed.
- [ ] Follow-up checkpoint date set.
