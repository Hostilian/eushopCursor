# Day-30 Closeout Checklist

Use this checklist to formally close the readiness program and hand over to post-launch operations.

## Final decision closure

- [ ] `go-no-go-decision.md` updated with final `GO` or `HOLD`.
- [ ] All required sign-offs are recorded.
- [ ] Conditions and follow-up checkpoints documented.

## Evidence closure

- [ ] `evidence-log.md` contains final gate evidence.
- [ ] Rehearsal and drill reports are finalized.
- [ ] KPI snapshot for the final week is recorded.
- [ ] Risk register is updated with final statuses.

## Action closure

- [ ] All active readiness actions are either done or migrated.
- [ ] Remaining active actions moved to post-launch plan.
- [ ] Owners and due dates confirmed for migrated items.

## Documentation closure

- [ ] `day30-exec-summary.md` finalized and published.
- [ ] `artifact-status-board.md` reflects final statuses.
- [ ] Weekly review record for the final week is done.

## Transition handoff

- [ ] Post-launch transition template done.
- [ ] On-call/incident ownership confirmed for launch +7 days.
- [ ] First post-launch review meeting set to `ready`.
