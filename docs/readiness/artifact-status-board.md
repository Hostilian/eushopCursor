## Status meanings

- `done`: delivered and ready for use.
- `in_progress`: execution started; waiting on final evidence/sign-off.
- `ready`: execution packet finalized and pre-assigned for scheduled window.
- `blocked`: cannot proceed until blocker is resolved.
