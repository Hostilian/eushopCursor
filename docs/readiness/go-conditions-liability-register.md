## Rules

- No condition may be accepted without:
  - owner,
  - approver,
  - expiry date,
  - mitigation plan.
- Expired items must be closed or explicitly renewed in writing.
- Every active item must be referenced from `go-no-go-decision.md`.

## Review cadence

- Review at every weekly readiness meeting until closed.
- Reconfirm at first post-launch review for any still-in_progress item.
