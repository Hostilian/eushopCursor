# 30-Day GTM And Customer Success Playbook

## Goal

Launch with consistent value messaging, realistic scope promises, and support readiness.

## Positioning pillars

- Trust-first peer logistics for diaspora corridors.
- Real journey utility: trip offers, open asks, and local listings.
- Privacy-respecting discovery and transparent platform role.

## Week-3 deliverables

- Launch narrative one-pager aligned with shipped features.
- Objection handling sheet for support and community ambassadors.
- Corridor prioritization checklist and activation plan.
- Customer support SLA and escalation protocol.

## Message house

| Audience | Core message | Proof point | Do not claim |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Buyers | Find trusted people already traveling your route | Live trip/listing flows and chat | Guaranteed delivery or customs handling |
| Bringers/sellers | Monetize spare luggage and local availability | Reservation and payout flow where enabled | Universal in-app settlement in every surface |
| Community partners | Build local corridor liquidity safely | Moderation + trust workflows | Unlimited moderation capacity |

## Customer success SLA

| Ticket severity | First response target | Resolution target | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| P0 | <= 1 hour | Same day containment | CS lead + on-call engineer |
| P1 | <= 4 hours | Next business day | CS lead |
| P2 | <= 1 business day | Planned batch | CS team |

## Launch checklist

- [ ] Narrative claims match current product behavior on web/mobile.
- [ ] Legal copy and operational contact channels are set in deploy env.
- [ ] Support macros and escalation tree are published.
- [ ] Known limitations are documented in internal FAQ.

## Metrics

- Activation funnel completion for pilot corridors.
- First response SLA adherence.
- Ticket reopen rate.
- Weekly corridor-level retention/repeat signal.
