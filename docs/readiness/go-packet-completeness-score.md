# GO Packet Completeness Score

Score readiness packet completeness before the final meeting.

## Scoring model

- Each required artifact = 10 points
- Maximum = 100 points
- Suggested thresholds:
  - `>= 90`: Eligible for GO discussion
  - `70-89`: Conditional HOLD until gaps close
  - `< 70`: HOLD
