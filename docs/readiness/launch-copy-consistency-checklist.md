# Launch Copy Consistency Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure launch messaging matches real product state.

## Product claim alignment

- [ ] Public claims match shipped capabilities.
- [ ] Known limitations are explicitly stated where needed.
- [ ] Mobile/web parity caveats are correctly represented.

## Legal and trust language

- [ ] Terms/privacy wording aligns with actual flows.
- [ ] Payments wording does not over-claim scope.
- [ ] Trust/safety wording matches current moderation operations.

## Channel consistency

- [ ] Website and GTM content use consistent core claims.
- [ ] Support macros match latest product behavior.
- [ ] Executive brief and public messaging do not conflict.

## Sign-off

- [ ] Product lead reviewed.
- [ ] GTM lead reviewed.
- [ ] Legal/privacy reviewer consulted when needed.
