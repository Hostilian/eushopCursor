# 🇪🇺 EuShop Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: July 1, 2026**

EuShop ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data when you use the EuShop platform, in accordance with the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (Regulation (EU) 2016/679).

---

## 1. Data Controller

For the purposes of the GDPR, the data controller is:
* **Entity**: EuShop OÜ / EuShop s.r.o. (in formation)
* **Email**: privacy@eushop.eu

---

## 2. Personal Data We Collect

We collect data to provide, improve, and secure our peer-to-peer marketplace services:
* **Account Registration Data**: Username, email address, password hash, and language preference.
* **Listing Details**: Freeform listing descriptions, country ISO codes, approximate city location, and finder's fees.
* **Geocoding & Location Data**: General location coordinates (lat/lng) representing your city or neighborhood to display listings on the map. We do not collect precise live background GPS data unless consented to.
* **Contact Information**: Contact handles (e.g. email, phone, WhatsApp) that you choose to provide to buyers.
* **Cookies & Session Identifiers**: Temporary session cookies for authentication (Better Auth) and preference tracking.

---

## 3. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following GDPR lawful bases:
* **Contractual Necessity (Art. 6(1)(b))**: To create your account, manage listings, and handle platform features.
* **Consent (Art. 6(1)(a))**: For optional cookies, marketing communications, and location sharing.
* **Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f))**: For platform security, abuse prevention, analytics, and debugging.

---

## 4. How We Share Your Data

* **Public Listings**: Any food listings you publish (including your item description, city, and contact details) are publicly visible on the map. Do not post private address details.
* **Third-Party Providers**: We share metadata with necessary processors who comply with GDPR guidelines:
  * **Better Auth** (User Authentication data)
  * **Mapbox / Leaflet** (To geocode and render listing maps)
  * **PostHog** (Aggregated, anonymized UI metrics)
  * **Stripe** (Secure payment metadata and KYC checks)

---

## 5. Your Rights Under GDPR

As an EU resident, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:
* **Right of Access**: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
* **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
* **Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")**: Request deletion of your account and all associated listings.
* **Right to Portability**: Export your listings and profile data in a machine-readable format.
* **Right to Lodge a Complaint**: Complain to your local supervisory authority (e.g. the Office for Personal Data Protection in the Czech Republic).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at `privacy@eushop.eu`. We will respond within 30 days.

---

## 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to maintain your login session. You can manage your preferences through our Cookie Consent Banner. Our tracking uses anonymized IDs to analyze usage without linking to your real-world identity.
