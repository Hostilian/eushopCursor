# Voice glossary (Eushop)

| Term | Use when | Do not use for |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| **Trip offer** | A published route with dates and suitcase **slots** | A single pantry item |
| **Slot / reservation** | Buyer locks capacity on a trip | Listing purchase |
| **Agreed fee / slot fee** | Money the buyer pays the traveller for a **trip** slot (UI copy) | Avoid “finder’s fee” here (schema may still say `agreedFinderFee`) |
| **Finder’s fee** | **Pantry listings** and **open requests** (neighbour-to-neighbour taste-of-home) | Trip slot pricing |
| **Platform fee** | Eushop’s cut on a **confirmed** reservation (`min(€1.50, 12% × base)`) | The traveller’s payout |
| **Listing** | Surplus food posted near you with pickup | Trip |
| **Request** | Buyer asks for an item; max finder fee optional | Trip announcement |
| **Cell / 5 km** | Privacy geohash-5 discovery | Exact address |
| **Corridor** | Route-first discovery between cities | Only “country filter” |

**Product order (README & pitch):** trip offers → open requests → pantry listings (unless a page is listing-first by context).

**Banned / tighten:** unsourced “two hundred million”, “sixteen million” without definition; “Euromonitor 2024” in historical claims; duplicate opening stats across About vs Manifesto vs README.
