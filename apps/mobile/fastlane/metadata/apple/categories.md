# App Store categories

Set in **App Store Connect → App Information**. Not derived from the metadata text files.

- **Primary:** Travel
- **Secondary:** Lifestyle

Rationale: The trip-offer primitive (spare luggage capacity on existing routes) is fundamentally a travel utility. Local listings and open asks are the lifestyle-discovery secondary use case. Avoid Shopping (regulated) and Social Networking (App Review treats those much more strictly than Travel).

If the secondary slot becomes available, prefer **Lifestyle** over **Social Networking** until UGC moderation maturity is documented in `docs/readiness/`.
